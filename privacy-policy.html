Privacy Policy & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

LIBSEE MARKETPLACE LTD ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LIBSEE mobile application. This page outlines our Privacy Policy and Cancellation & Refund Policy regarding the collection, use, and protection of your data and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, gender, age, height, and weight (optional, for size recommendations).
  • Location Data: Your device's location, used to show nearby shops and calculate distances. Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time.
  • Wishlist & Orders: Products you save, search for, or purchase through the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the LIBSEE service
  • To show relevant shops and products based on your location
  • To process orders and deliver items
  • To personalize product recommendations
  • To communicate important service updates

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — backend database and authentication
  • OpenStreetMap — map display
  • Google Fonts — font rendering

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw location permission at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at libseeco@gmail.com.

6. Cancellation and Refund Policy

6.1 Order Cancellation

Customers may cancel their order within 3 hours of placing it through the application.

6.2 Refunds

If an order is canceled within the 3-hour window, a full refund will be issued to the original payment method.

6.3 Orders Under Processing

After 3 hours, the order is forwarded to the merchant for processing and can no longer be canceled through the application.

6.4 Defective or Incorrect Products

If a customer receives a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, they must contact customer support within 48 hours of delivery. After review, an appropriate resolution will be provided, which may include a refund, replacement, or other solution.

6.5 Marketplace Platform

LIBSEE MARKETPLACE LTD operates as a marketplace platform connecting customers with independent merchants. Responsibility for product availability, quality, fulfillment, and delivery lies with the respective merchants.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
