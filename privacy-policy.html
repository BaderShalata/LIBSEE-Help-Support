Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

LIBSEE ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LIBSEE mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, gender, age, height, and weight (optional, for size recommendations).
  • Location Data: Your device's location, used to show nearby shops and calculate distances. Location access is optional and can be disabled at any time from your device settings.
  • Wishlist & Orders: Products you save, search for, or purchase through the app.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the LIBSEE service
  • To show you relevant shops and products based on your location
  • To process orders and deliver items
  • To personalize product recommendations
  • To communicate important service updates

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

LIBSEE uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — backend database and authentication
  • OpenStreetMap — map display
  • Google Fonts — font rendering

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw location permission at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at shalatab4@gmail.com.
