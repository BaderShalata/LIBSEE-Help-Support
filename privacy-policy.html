Privacy Policy & Refund Policy

Libsee Marketplace LTD operates the LIBSEE mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding data collection, usage, cancellation, and refunds.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone, gender, age, height, weight (optional)
  • Location Data: used for nearby shops
  • Wishlist & Orders
  • Device Information

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide service
  • Show nearby shops
  • Process orders
  • Recommendations
  • Updates

3. Data Security

Stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell data.

4. Third Party Services

  • Supabase
  • OpenStreetMap
  • Google Fonts

5. Your Rights

  • Access data
  • Correct data
  • Delete account
  • Disable location

6. Cancellation and Refund Policy – Libsee

1. Order Cancellation

Customers may cancel their order within 3 hours from the time the order was placed through the application.

2. Refunds

If the order is canceled within 3 hours, a full refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

3. Orders Under Processing

After 3 hours from the time of purchase, the order is forwarded to the merchant for processing and can no longer be canceled through the application.

4. Defective or Incorrect Products

If a customer receives a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, they must contact customer support within 48 hours of receiving the order. After review, an appropriate refund or resolution will be provided.

7. Disclaimer

Libsee is not responsible for product quality, availability, fulfillment, or delivery. Merchants are fully responsible.
