Privacy Policy & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Libsee Marketplace LTD ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LIBSEE mobile application. This page outlines our policies regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal data, as well as our cancellation and refund terms.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, gender, age, height, and weight (optional).
  • Location Data: Used to show nearby shops and calculate distances. This is optional and can be disabled anytime.
  • Wishlist & Orders: Products you save, search for, or purchase.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, and app version for diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the LIBSEE service
  • To show relevant shops and products
  • To process orders and deliveries
  • To personalize recommendations
  • To send important service updates

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We use industry-standard encryption and do not sell your personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — backend & authentication
  • OpenStreetMap — maps
  • Google Fonts — typography

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Withdraw location permission anytime

Contact: support@libsee.co

6. Cancellation and Refund Policy

6.1 Order Cancellation

Customers may cancel their order within 3 hours of placing it through the application.

6.2 Refunds

If canceled within 3 hours, a full refund will be issued to the original payment method.

6.3 Orders Under Processing

After 3 hours, the order is forwarded to the merchant for processing and can no longer be canceled.

6.4 Defective or Incorrect Products

If a customer receives a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, they must contact support within 48 hours of delivery. A refund, replacement, or resolution may be provided after review.

7. Disclaimer

Libsee operates as a platform connecting customers with independent merchants. We are not responsible for product quality, availability, accuracy of listings, fulfillment, or delivery services provided by merchants. All responsibility for products lies with the respective merchants.
